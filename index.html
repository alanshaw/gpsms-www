GPSMS

An iPhone app that allows you to send your current location, pinpointed on a map, to a friend. Available soon from the iPhone App Store.

My location in your inbox, your location in mine

"We're at the pub, see you soon?"
"I'm at work. Meet me outside for lunch."
"Fantastic pizza here. You should check it out!"


Why go through the trouble of explaining where you are? Surely there are more important things to be said. This is what GPSMS is made for: communicating your location.

GPS + SMS = GPSMS

GPSMS is essentially a short text message and your location from anywhere in the world, sent from one mobile to another. The app simply displays both these types of information on your iPhone.

Send your location to a friend or group of friends

You can send your location to as many of your address book contacts as you like. Adding contacts is as simple as typing their name and selecting them from the list of matching names that appear.

Hell, send to anyone!

If some or all of the people you want to send a GPSMS message to haven't installed the GPSMS app yet, you can send them an SMS or email containing a link to your location on Google Maps.

Simple and elegant interface

We've used the simple and familiar look and feel of the Apple Mail app so you'll feel at home using GPSMS.

Push notifications

Receive notifications of new GPSMS messages when you aren't using the app.

Find your way

GPSMS makes it easy to get to somewhere. The map view drops a red pin to show where the sender sent the GPSMS from and a green pin to show where you currently are.
